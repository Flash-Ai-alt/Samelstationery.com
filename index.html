About SAMEL PRINTERS STATIONERY

Welcome to SAMEL PRINTERS STATIONERY — Lusaka's trusted destination for high-quality stationery and professional printing services. We are passionate about helping students, businesses, and organizations find exactly what they need.

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a comprehensive stationery and printing hub, serving hundreds of satisfied customers across Lusaka and Zambia. Our goal is simple: provide top-quality products and services at affordable prices, delivered with a smile.

Whether you're restocking your office, printing corporate documents, or preparing a child for a new school term — we're here for you, every step of the way.
